About Me

Photo by Scott Faber; ScottFaber.com

First exposure to code all started back in middle school with editing xanga and myspace to individualize my pages. Ever since middle school I have dabbled on and off with coding. I enjoy learning about coding as a hobby. I'm a knowledgeable Full-Stack Developer with several years of programming experience who is comfortable with programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I thoroughly enjoy Full-Stack Web Development, but I am most comfortable with Front-End Development. Extensive exposure to HTML and CSS for several years during my tenure as a Computer Programmer Assistant while attending The University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill. I have further developed my skills with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Git, Bootstrap, Materialize, APIs, Node.js, Firebase, MySQL, MongoDB, Express.js, Handlebars.js, React.js, C#, ASP.NET, Material-UI, Balsamiq, Passport, Zenhub, and Heroku in the higher education sector. I'm also a passionate learner, who enjoys being a team player, participating in research, with a robust interest in science, technology, art, the environment, and animals. Excellent attention to detail and team collaboration for executing new projects.
