About Me

Photo by Scott Faber; ScottFaber.com

First exposure to code all started back in middle school with editing xanga and myspace to individualize my pages. Ever since middle school I have dabbled on and off with coding, but now I have taken the full plunge into pursuing it as a career. I'm a knowledgeable Full-Stack Developer with several years of programming experience who is comfortable with programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I thoroughly enjoy Full-Stack Web Development, but I am most comfortable with Front-End Development. Extensive exposure to HTML and CSS for several years during my tenure as a Computer Programmer Assistant while attending The University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill. I have further developed my skills with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Materialize, JavaScript, jQuery, APIs, Node.js, Firebase, MySQL, Express.js, Handlebars.js, MongoDB, C#, React.js, and ASP.NET in the higher education sector. I'm also a passionate learner, who enjoys being a team player, participating in research, with a robust interest in science, technology, art, the environment, and animals. Excellent attention to detail and team collaboration for executing new projects.
